# Apple News+ Story Recirculation Project

## Executive Summary

This project helps the Newsroom AI and Audience teams increase Apple News+ traffic by turning proven older stories into a daily shortlist of evergreen re-share candidates. The system uses historical performance signals to surface the stories most likely to generate additional pageviews without asking the newsroom to produce new content.

## Why It Matters

Apple News gives the team a limited daily republish opportunity. By using AI to identify stories with the best chance of succeeding on that platform, the team can drive more engagement, more partner value, and more revenue from content that already exists.

## How It Works

Each day, the workflow reviews the eligible story pool, filters out pieces that are too recent, too old, or too successful to need another pass, and then recommends 5 to 10 stories for the audience editor to choose from.

## Success Metric

The goal is to increase the number of stories shared through the Apple News evergreen channel, moving from roughly 1 per week to 5 per day, while improving total engagement minutes on Apple News+.
